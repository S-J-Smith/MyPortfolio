Introduction


I am Steve and I am 33 Years old and live and work in the UK. I have been working in IT now for over 11 years and for the most part with a large International law firm Where I have gained the majority of my knowledge and experience within the Profession. I am now finding that I have had enough working as an Application Support Specialist and have decided to study in my own time from my own pocket to become a Web Developer. Since exploring my options I found that I really enjoy learning web development and the creative element it has to it and also like the flexible lifestyle that can go with it which better suits who I am and who I want to be.

This site will act as my Portfolio and with the aim of this being an eventual main point of reference for future employment and/or work in the field and therefore my goal here is to have a fully functional interactive, colourful and accessible website to showcase my skills and experience along my journey to become the best web developer I can possibly be!
